Belgrade Beer Fest

The Belgrade Beer Fest is an annual festival of beer in Belgrade, Serbia. Started in 2003, the festival is held annually over five days each August as a showcase event for verious beer producers. The festival entrance is free.

  • 2003-2007 Kalemegdan fortress
